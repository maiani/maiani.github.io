Andrea Maiani

Postdocoral associate at CQIP, New York University

I am a postdoctoral associate at NYU Center for Quantum Information Physics. I specialized in quantum nanoelectronics, mesoscopic superconductivity and quantum technologies.

Research areas

Hybrid heterostructures

My main interest is studying nanoelectronic devices composed by different materials, mainly superconductors and semiconductors.

Quantum devices

I study superconducting circuits to implement quantum information processing.

Vortex nucleation

I worked on estimating the energy barrier experienced by vortices that nucleate in superconductors.
